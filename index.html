Pet Care Tips & Guides

Quick reads to keep your companion healthy, happy, and thriving.

🐶
Dogs
How Often Should You Bathe Your Dog?
Most dogs need a bath every 4–6 weeks. Over-bathing strips natural oils and can irritate skin — less is often more.
🐱
Cats
Signs Your Cat Is Actually Happy
Slow blinks, a raised tail, and kneading are all signs of deep feline contentment. Learn to read your cat's body language.
🐰
Small Pets
What Rabbits Should (and Shouldn't) Eat
Unlimited hay is the foundation. Pellets in moderation, fresh greens daily, and absolutely no iceberg lettuce.
